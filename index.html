Stansted Airport Drop Off: What You Need to Know

Avoid unexpected fines! Learn about official drop-off charges, free alternatives, and smart travel tips for stress free arrivals at London Stansted Airport.

Understanding Stansted Airport Drop Off

If you're planning to drop someone off at London Stansted Airport (STN), it’s essential to understand the current rules and fees. Since 2019, Stansted has enforced a strict policy: all private vehicle drop off must use the designated Short Stay car park there is no longer a free drop-off zone directly outside the terminal.

This change was implemented to reduce congestion, improve safety, and streamline traffic flow. While convenient, this system comes with a cost and potential fines if you don’t comply. In this comprehensive guide, we’ll break down everything you need to know about Stansted Airport drop off procedures, charges, legal alternatives, and how to save money.

Stansted Airport Drop Off Charges

As of 2026, the official drop-off fee at Stansted Airport is £7 for up to 15 minutes in the Short Stay car park. This charge applies to all private vehicles, including cars, vans, and ride share services like Uber or Bolt.

  • Payment method: Must be made online before or immediately after arrival via the Stansted Airport website or at pay stations.
  • Late payment penalty: Failure to pay within the grace period results in a Penalty Charge Notice (PCN) of £70 (reduced to £35 if paid within 14 days).
  • Time limit: You are allowed only 15 minutes from entry to exit. Exceeding this may incur additional fees.

Note: The charge is per visit not per passenger. So whether you’re dropping off one person or four, the fee remains £7.

Official Rules for Drop-Offs at Stansted

Stansted Airport enforces strict rules to maintain order and safety:

  • Stopping on the terminal forecourt (outside departures) is **prohibited** and monitored by ANPR cameras.
  • All vehicles must enter the Short Stay car park (follow signs for “Short Stay” or “Drop-Off”).
  • Passengers must be dropped off on Level 1 of the Short Stay car park, which connects directly to the terminal via a covered walkway (2–3 minute walk).
  • Taxis and pre booked minicabs may use the dedicated taxi rank without paying the drop-off fee but private drivers cannot.

Free Drop-Off Alternatives Near Stansted

Looking to avoid the £7 fee? Several legal, free alternatives exist:

1. Use the Stansted Express Pick-Up Point

While primarily for rail passengers, the station area allows brief stops. However, enforcement is increasing use with caution and only for very quick drop-offs.

2. Book a Pre Arranged Minicab

Reputable minicab services (like those from Hendon, Kingsbury, or Edgware) are licensed to use the airport’s taxi facilities without incurring the drop-off charge. Your driver drops you curbside legally.

3. Park & Ride via Nearby Locations

Some travelers arrange to be dropped off at nearby locations like Bishop’s Stortford or take a local bus into the airport. While less convenient, it eliminates the fee entirely.

Smart Tips to Save on Stansted Drop-Offs

  • Pre-book your drop-off: Some third party parking services offer discounted rates when bundled with other services.
  • Use public transport: The Stansted Express from London Liverpool Street is fast (47 mins) and avoids road fees entirely.
  • Share rides: Coordinate with other travelers heading to the airport to split the £7 cost.
  • Choose a minicab: Services from areas like Hendon, Kingsbury, or Edgware offer fixed fares and legal curbside drop-offs.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is there a free drop-off at Stansted Airport?

No. All private vehicles must pay £7 to use the Short Stay car park for drop-offs.

How long can I stay in the drop-off zone?

You have up to 15 minutes from entry to exit. Exceeding this may result in extra charges.

Can taxis drop off for free?

Yes. Licensed taxis and pre-booked minicabs can use the official taxi rank without paying the drop-off fee.

What happens if I don’t pay the drop-off charge?

You’ll receive a £70 Penalty Charge Notice (reduced to £35 if paid within 14 days).
